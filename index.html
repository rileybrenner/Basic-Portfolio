About Me

Welcome to my page! My name is Riley Brenner. I am a 26 year old software developer who is currently located in Berkeley, California. I am originally from a small beach town in New York outside of Queens called Long Beach.

My undegrad studies were at the University of North Carolina at Wilmington. Wilmington is a port city on the southern coast of North Carolina. I have a Bachelor of Science Degree in Business with a concentration in Marketing & Advertising.

I enjoy spending every waking moment of my free time outside. My passion in life is surfing. I started at an early age in elementary school in the temperate waters of Northeastern United States. Some of my other hobbies include hiking, snowboarding, paddle boarding, diving and spear fishing.
